# Code of Conduct

## Our Promise

We want iloveAgents to be a welcoming place for everyone.
No matter who you are, where you're from, or how much experience you have —
you deserve to be treated with respect here.

## What We Expect

Be kind. Be helpful. Be patient.

Good behavior looks like:
- Being friendly and welcoming to new contributors
- Respecting different opinions and ideas
- Giving honest, helpful feedback
- Owning your mistakes and learning from them

Bad behavior looks like:
- Insulting or attacking other people
- Harassment of any kind
- Sharing someone's private information without permission
- Any behavior that makes others feel unwelcome or unsafe

## If Something Goes Wrong

If someone is behaving badly, please report it privately to **[YOUR EMAIL HERE]**.

I will take every report seriously and handle it fairly.
